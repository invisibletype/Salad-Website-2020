How Salad Works

Install

Trust is the biggest hurdle here: ‘run this application and we’ll pay you’ is a radically new proposition and sounds like a scam.

Salad’s application is open source and supported by a strong, vocal community. Many believe blockchain is a virus or damaging, Salad is changing this misconception with our 4.7 star Trustpilot rating.

Selecting, configuring & maintaining multiple mining libraries is labor intensive. Switching protocols based on spot-profitability requires constant attention and each coin/token requires delicate custodial work. The mass market can’t be bothered.

Salad helps by selecting the most profitable workload, providing a custodial service and liquidity, all with no KYC.

‘Run this miner for a month and you’ll get 0.082364 ETH’ …we know this doesn’t appeal to the mass market.

Except for ideological early adopters, blockchain simply doesn’t resonate. Our incentive model is the key to Salad’s success, users want our curated rewards: subscriptions, games, gift cards, hardware and money to spend online - this actually motivates.
